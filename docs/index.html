CastLocalVideos

Cast receiver host. This page exists so Chromecast devices can fetch the CastLocalVideos styled receiver UI over HTTPS.

The receiver itself is at /receiver.html and is loaded by your TV — not meant to be opened in a regular browser.

Looking for the app? CastLocalVideos is a desktop app — install it on your Mac, Windows, or Linux machine and play your local videos through a Chromecast on your TV.
